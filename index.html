HOW TO PLAY
  1. The timeline has 3 anchor games pre-placed as reference points — oldest at the top, newest at the bottom.
  2. Drag games from the pool into the right spot on the timeline. On mobile, press and hold to drag.
  3. Place every game on the timeline, then hit Check Timeline.
  4. Correct games lock in and turn green. Wrong ones turn red — move them and check again.
  5. You get 3 checks total to get every game in the right spot.
