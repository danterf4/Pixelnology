🎮 HOW TO PLAY
  1. The timeline is split into 3 eras — Early, Mid, and Modern. Each era has its own anchor game pre-placed.
  2. Drag games from the pool at the bottom into the correct era's timeline slot. On mobile, press and hold to drag.
  3. Place all 6 games in an era before hitting Check Timeline.
  4. If an entire era is in the right order, it locks and turns green — those games are done!
  5. Fix wrong games in incomplete eras and check again.
  6. Years are revealed only when an era is fully correct!
