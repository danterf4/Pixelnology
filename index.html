🎮 How to play ▾
  1. Drag a game from the pool and drop it into a slot on the timeline. On mobile, press and hold to drag.
  2. One game is pre-placed as an anchor. Build the timeline around it in chronological order (oldest → newest, top to bottom).
  3. Place all games before checking — you won't get feedback until every slot is filled.
  4. Hit Check Timeline. You'll be told how many are wrong, but not which ones.
  5. Drag games to new spots and check again until every game is in the right place.
  6. Years are revealed only when every game is in the correct spot!
