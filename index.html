HOW TO PLAY
  1. The timeline has 3 anchor games pre-placed. They divide the timeline into sections — oldest at the top, newest at the bottom.
  2. Drag games from the pool into the right spot on the timeline. On mobile, press and hold to drag.
  3. Place all games in a section before hitting Check Timeline.
  4. If a section is in the right order, it locks and turns green — those games are done!
  5. Fix wrong games in incomplete sections and check again.
  6. Years are revealed only when a section is fully correct!
