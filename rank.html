HOW TO PLAY
  1. The ranking has 2 anchor games pre-placed as reference points. Highest average score at the top, lowest at the bottom.
  2. The score is the average of each game's Metacritic Metascore (critics) and User Score — critics and players don't always agree!
  3. Drag games from the pool into the right spot in the ranking. On mobile, press and hold to drag.
  4. Rank every game, then hit Check ranking. Correct spots lock in and turn green, wrong ones turn red — move them and check again.
  5. You get 3 checks total to get every remaining game in its correct spot.
