I’ve always wanted to have a personal blog, and this seems like a great place to start. It’s not that I have anything particularly important to say, but I’ve always wanted a space to share my thoughts on the video games I’m playing—or even other forms of media I’m consuming.

Gameology began while I was playing *Hitster* (a board game about arranging songs on a timeline) with my family. I thought, "I’d love to play something like this, but with video games." And here we are. I lost my job a month ago, so this seemed like a good way to feel productive and avoid sinking into the despair of unemployment. Plus, I’ve always wanted to create a game featuring daily challenges—something quick and accessible for people to jump into.

If you’ve already played *Pixelnology* and found your way here, thank you so much for trying my little game and reading my thoughts. If you arrived here another way, thanks to you too—and go give *Pixelnology* a try; you might just like it! ^.^

I hope to write here every week. I should probably set up an email list so people can get notified whenever I publish a new post.
